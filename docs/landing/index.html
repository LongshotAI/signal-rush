Overview

Signal Rush is a gamified marketing platform built for the AI era. It turns the most underutilized screen real estate in tech — the terminal — into a high-engagement advertising surface that pays players in real API credits they can spend on their own AI workflows.

Our audience is the AI-first user: Hermes Agent operators, CLI natives, Telegram power users, and developers who already live in text-mode interfaces. They're highly technical, deeply engaged, and notoriously hard to reach through traditional advertising. Signal Rush meets them where they already are.

Advertisers get premium placement in a focused, distraction-free environment. Players get rewarded for their attention with API credits redeemable through VMCO.ai — no account binding, no strings attached. It's the first marketing channel that aligns incentives between brands and the AI-native audience.

03 — Architecture

Engine-first.
Deterministic. Testable.

The engine has zero I/O dependencies. It steps pure state. One core, read by many surfaces. That's how you ship games that work everywhere without rewriting everything.
